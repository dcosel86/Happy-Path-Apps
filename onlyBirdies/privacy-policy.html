Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use OnlyBirdies (the "App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Policy.

1. Information We Collect

When you use the App, we may collect the following types of information:

Account Information

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Password

Golf Data

  • Handicap
  • Golf rounds
  • Scores
  • Performance history

Device Information

  • Device model
  • Operating system
  • App version

Usage Data

  • Interactions with features within the App

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide core functionality such as score tracking and statistics
  • Improve the performance, features, and usability of the App
  • Sync data across your devices using our backend services
  • Send important notices, such as subscription updates or policy changes
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues or abuse

3. Subscriptions and Payments

Subscription purchases (monthly or yearly) are processed through Apple's in-app purchase system.

We do not store your payment details. Billing is managed securely by Apple.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, which may process your data in accordance with their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase (Google LLC) — for authentication, database, and analytics
  • Branch.io — for deep linking and marketing attribution
  • Apple StoreKit — for handling subscriptions and in-app purchases

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely in cloud databases. We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services.

If you delete your account, we will remove or anonymize your personal data within a reasonable time.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

If we learn that we have collected personal data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information
  • Opt-out of certain data processing activities
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, please contact us at: david@happypathapps.co

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be effective upon posting in the App.

Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised Policy.

10. Contact Us
